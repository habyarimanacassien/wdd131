Website Plan

Platform for my courses I teach

1. Site Name

Teach With Cassien

Why This Name?

The name is chosen to show a website where I provide course materials, weekly topics, assignments, and resources for students in the subjects I teach.

Optional domain: teachwithcassien.org

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an organized, easily accessible platform for my students to find information about their courses. The site will contain course descriptions, objectives, weekly topics, assignments, quizzes, announcements, and helpful resources. It will enhance communication and help students stay updated throughout the semester.

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I find weekly assignments and their due dates?
  • How do I access additional resources or readings for a topic?
  • What are the course objectives and grading criteria?
